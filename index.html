Legacy

Evolution of TECHELONS

From inception to innovationâ€”a journey of excellence and inspiration

TECHELONS launched in 2024 with a vision to create India's premier technical festival, bringing together brilliant minds from across the nation to showcase innovation and technical excellence. The inaugural event featured 500+ participants and 30+ competitions, establishing a strong foundation for what would become a national phenomenon.

By 2025, TECHELONS had scaled exponentially, welcoming 100+ institutions and 5000+ attendees. The event featured groundbreaking projects in emerging technologies including AI, Robotics, and Sustainable Engineering, with over 50+ competitions and workshops across multiple domains. Distinguished industry leaders and innovators graced the occasion, inspiring students to push boundaries and pioneer new solutions for real-world challenges.

Now in 2026, TECHELONS continues to evolve as we pioneer the next decade in technology. Join us as we create an ecosystem of collaboration, learning, and innovation that will shape tomorrow's world.
