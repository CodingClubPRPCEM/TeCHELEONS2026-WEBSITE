About TECHELONS 2025

TECHELONS 2025 marked a milestone year in our journey, bringing together brilliant minds from across the nation to showcase innovation and technical excellence.

The event featured groundbreaking projects in emerging technologies including AI, Robotics, and Sustainable Engineering, with over 50+ competitions and workshops.

Distinguished industry leaders and innovators graced the occasion, inspiring students to push boundaries and pioneer new solutions for real-world challenges.

With participation from 100+ institutions and thousands of passionate students, TECHELONS 2025 created an ecosystem of collaboration and learning.

The event's success reinforced our commitment to nurturing the next generation of tech pioneers and innovators who will shape tomorrow's world.
