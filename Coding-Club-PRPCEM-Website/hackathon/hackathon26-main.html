About TECHELONS 2026

The Ultimate National-Level Hackathon! TECHELONS 2026 challenges participants to build automation solutions that optimize workflows, eliminate manual bottlenecks, and boost productivity. Focus on AI, IoT, Robotics, and Smart Campus solutions.

AI & Robotics 🤖

Build intelligent systems using AI, Machine Learning, and Robotics that automate complex tasks and create smart automation solutions.

IoT & Cloud ☁️

Work in teams of up to 5 members. Create IoT solutions, cloud-based automation, and smart campus systems for enhanced connectivity.

Workflow Optimization ⚙️

Identify bottlenecks and create workflow automation solutions that boost productivity and deliver measurable performance improvements.
