FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I join the Coding Club?

You can register for Hackathon 26 by filling out our form through the "Hackathon 26" button or by attending one of our events. We welcome students from all branches and skill levels.

Are there any membership fees?

No, our club membership is completely free! We believe in making technology education accessible to everyone.

What programming languages do you focus on?

We cover a wide range of technologies including Python, JavaScript, Java, C++, React, Node.js, AI/ML frameworks, and many more based on industry demands and member interests.

Do you provide mentorship for beginners?

Absolutely! We have a dedicated mentorship program where experienced members guide beginners through their coding journey with personalized support.

How often do you organize events?

We organize events regularly - typically 2-3 workshops per month, quarterly hackathons, and monthly tech talks. Keep an eye on our events page for updates!
